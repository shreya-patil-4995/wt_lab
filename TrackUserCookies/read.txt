How to test the system during your exam:

Start Apache and MySQL from your XAMPP Control Panel.
Go to your browser and open http://localhost/TrackUserCookies/.
It will redirect to the Login page. Click on the "Register" link at the bottom.
Create a new user account.
Log in with the newly created account.
Check your Dashboard. It will state that you're using Sessions and have active Tracking Cookies.
Close the browser and reopen the URL; the cookies should automatically log you back in.
Click "Logout" to terminate both the session and the cookies.
The code is straightforward, strictly addressing the requirements without any complicated frameworks. Good luck with your lab exam! Let me know if you need any adjustments to the code.
